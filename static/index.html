      Why connect your mail?

      Connecting your SMTP server allows all candidate outreach and personalized rejection reports to be dispatched directly from your email account, ensuring maximum trust and inbox deliverability.

      How to configure Gmail:

      1. Enable 2-Step Verification on your Google Account.
      2. Go to Google Account Security.
      3. Search for "App passwords" in the search bar.
      4. Generate a password called "AI Recruiter".
      5. Copy the 16-character code and paste it in the form.
      Crucial: Do not enter your normal Gmail password. Google blocks standard login attempts. You must use an App Password.
